About M4v3r1cX

Weird composition, bad sound design and terrible mixing is what defines M4v3r1cX, an indie producer from a forgotten country at the edge of the world, who tries to blend genres and styles from between various sounds from electronica, drum and bass, house, synthwave, rock, and metal tracks.
M4v3r1cX's been playing guitar for a while but still isn't good. M4v3r1cX's a computer scientist by trade and currently works as a software architect.
The fuck modern web design joke is a joke but I really hate overbloated websites.
